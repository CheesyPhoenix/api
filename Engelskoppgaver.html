What is an app? (innlevering)

An app or application is a piece of software usually designed to be run on a digital device. An example of an app is wikipedia where you can search for topics that you want to know more about and presents you with information about said topic. Wikipedia is an app which you would normally download to a smartphone or other mobile device through the internet.

Bangalore: India’s Cybercity

Why are headhunters so interested in Bangalore?

Headhunters are so interested in bangalore because Indian workers earn only half or a third of what a british employee would earn, and it is therefore much easier to start a business in Bangalore than in Britain. There are also way fewer worker protection laws and workers can therefore easily be fired and replaced if they do not meet expectations.

How are Indian call center workers trained to deal with clients in the West?

Indian call center workers are trained to speak the language and dialect of the people in the target country as well as use common names from said country. In addition they are also taught basic general information about the country.
